About Women in Math

Our mission is to create a supportive community for women in mathematics

Women in Math (WIM) is a student-led organization at the University of Ottawa dedicated to supporting and empowering women pursuing mathematics. We provide a platform for networking, mentorship, and professional development.

Community Building

Creating a supportive network of women in mathematics through social events and gatherings.

Professional Development

Offering workshops, seminars, and networking events with industry professionals.

Mentorship

Connecting students with mentors in academia and industry for guidance and support.
