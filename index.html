About Women in Math

Women in Math (WiM) is a vibrant community dedicated to creating a more inclusive and welcoming space for anyone passionate about mathematics.

Women in Math is a student-led organization at the University of Ottawa dedicated to supporting and empowering women in mathematics or that simply enjoy mathematics. Our mission is to celebrate diversity in math and empower students through connection, support, and opportunity. At WiM, we believe math is for everyone. Join us to meet like-minded peers, explore exciting pathways in math and science, and be part of a community that values your voice and ideas!

Community Building

Creating a supportive network of women in mathematics through social events and gatherings.

Professional Development

Offering workshops, seminars, and networking events with industry professionals.

Mentorship

Connecting students with mentors in academia and industry for guidance and support.
